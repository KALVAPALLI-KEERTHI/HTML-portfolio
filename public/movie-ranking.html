The Best Movies According to Keerthi

My Top 3 Favourite Movies of all-time.

Sakhi

I hear evergreen music from this movie. The movie shows the south indian culture, tradition and pure mesmerizing love between two young souls. Youth enjoys this movie, its full of feelings and emotion that we can relate.

Bommarillu

Siddarath and Genelia are amazing actors, they gave life to their characters. Songs in the movie are next level. It bascically shows Indian parenthood. Always a favourite!!

Robo

Its full of technology.I really appreciate the director for having a great vision back in 2010 about robos. Must say, Aishwarya looks heavenly beautiful!!
